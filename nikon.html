Customer Reviews

The Nikon D780 DSLT Camera with 24-120 mm Lens Kit with advanced features can turn you into a professional photographer with fine quality snapshots at every click. Express yourself with the D780. Experience high resolution and great low light shooting capabilities with 24.5 effective megapixels and an ISO of up to 51200 thanks to the backside illumination CMOS sensor and EXPEED 6 image-processing engine. Great for astrophotography, this DSLR delivers extended long exposure through shutter speed extensions of up to 900 s. Its 8.12cm (3.2 inch) LCD display and 2359000dot resolution. Also its 5x zoom makes it easy to capture stunning landscapes, portraits, wedding photos and distant subjects when travelling.
